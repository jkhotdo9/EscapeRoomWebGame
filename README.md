# EscapeRoomWebGame

House of Souls 🏚️ 👻 🔪
 
Creators: Lily Hoopes, Christiannel Maningat, Victor Tang, Jin Kwak

Description: Lily wakes up in a cold, abandoned house — with no memories, no way out. The house belongs to a notorious serial killer, and somewhere in the dark, they're coming back. The only way to escape? Dig through the rooms, uncover the killer's twisted puzzles, and piece together the clues before time runs out. But she's not alone. Three other survivors are trapped inside, and together they'll have to navigate the house room by room. The clock is ticking. The killer is close. Get out before they gain a new victim.

Controls: WASD or Arrow Keys, E for interaction, I for inventory, LMB for clicking and dragging

Game Link: https://lilyhoopes.github.io/EscapeRoomWebGame/
